Basketball News

James Harden became 9-th player in NBA history scored 50 points in 10 games.

Point guard of “Houston Rockets” James Harden scored 54 points in game of NBA regular season with “Washington Wizards” (131:135 OT).

For James Harden it is 10-th 50 point game. Earlier only 8 players had this achievement.

NBA players with minimum 10 50-point games:

1. Wilt Chamberlain (118).

2. Michael Jordan (31).

3. Kobe Bryant (25).

4. Elgin Baylor (17).

5. Rick Barry (14).

6. Lebron James (12).

7. Allen Iverson (11).

8. Kareem Abdul-Jabbar (10)

9. James Harden (10).
