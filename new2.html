Shooting guard of “Golden State Warriors” Klay Tompson in game with “Chicago Bulls” broke the 3-point throws NBA record.

“Golden State Warriors” won “Chicago Bulls” 149-124. Klay Tompson was the most effective player of the game, scoring 52 points. Klay hit 14 throws out of 24, breaking the previous record of his teammate Stephen Curry (13).
